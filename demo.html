Installation Instructions

Follow these steps to install the extension in your browser:

For Chrome/Edge/Brave:

  1. Clone or download this repository to your computer
  2. Open your browser and go to chrome://extensions/
  3. Enable "Developer mode" in the top-right corner
  4. Click "Load unpacked"
  5. Select the Price-Check folder
  6. Done! The extension is now active

For Firefox:

  1. Clone or download this repository to your computer
  2. Open Firefox and go to about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox
  3. Click "Load Temporary Add-on"
  4. Navigate to the Price-Check folder and select manifest.json
  5. Done! The extension is now active

Using the Extension:

  1. Visit any product page on Amazon, Booking, or eBay
  2. The extension automatically detects and tracks the price
  3. Click the extension icon to see price history and analysis
  4. Add products to your wishlist for price drop alerts
  5. Configure settings in the extension popup

Privacy & Data

All data is stored locally in your browser. The extension does not send any information to external servers. Your shopping history, tracked prices, and wishlist remain completely private on your device.

You can clear all data at any time from the Settings tab in the extension popup.
